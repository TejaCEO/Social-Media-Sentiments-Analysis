About Us

We are a team of computer science enthusiasts working on a project to understand how people feel and share their opinions on social media

Using technology, we analyze online conversations to find out what people think and how they express their emotions.

Our goal is to learn more about what people say online and how it impacts others. With this project, we hope to provide clear insights into what’s happening on social media.
